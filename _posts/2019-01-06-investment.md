# Invest in equity 

## What is a good platform for investment in equity? 

- Open a demat account. (where your shares get stored)
- Open a trading account. (where your money gets stored)
- NSDL is National Securities Depository Limited. It deals with National Stock Exchange (NSE). 
- CDSL is Central Depository Services Limited. It deals with Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE).
- NSDL depository reaches its services to investors through market intermediaries called Depository Participants (DP)
- Demat A/c number - for NSDL and CDSL is 16 digits
- DPID - first 8 digits of demat a/c number
- Client ID (investor account) - is the second set of 8 digits
