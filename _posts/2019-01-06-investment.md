## What is a good platform for investment in equity? 

- Open a demat account. (where your shares get stored)
  - Demat A/c number - for NSDL and CDSL is 16 digits
  - DPID - first 8 digits of demat a/c number
  - Client ID (investor account) - is the second set of 8 digits 
- Open a trading account. (where your money gets stored)
