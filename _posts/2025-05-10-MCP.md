## Model Context Protocol (MCP)

1. Released in Nov 2024
2. open standard developed by **Anthropic** 
3. provides a consistent way for AI agents to connect with external **tools**, **services**, and **data**

4. [Core architecture of MCP](https://modelcontextprotocol.io/docs/concepts/architecture)
    1. Hosts are LLM applications (like Claude Desktop or IDEs) that initiate connections
    2. Clients maintain 1:1 connections with servers, inside the host application
    3. Servers provide context, tools, and prompts to clients

MCP Toolbox for Databases was formerly called **Gen AI Toolbox for Databases**

1. While the toolbox is often showcased with Google Cloud databases like BigQuery and AlloyDB, it is designed to work with over 15 database types, including Snowflake

1. Dont just have LLM create a query. Use templated queries. 
1. **Templated Queries**: You can define specific SQL queries in a tools.yaml file, allowing AI agents to call them with simple parameters (e.g., date ranges) instead of writing raw SQL.
